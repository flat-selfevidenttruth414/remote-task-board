## 📌 Overview

Remote-task-board helps teams track work. It organizes tasks in a simple visual layout. You see what work requires attention. You track progress across projects. This tool assists remote workers who need a central place for project updates. It removes the guesswork from team collaboration.

## 🛠 Features

*   **Task Lists:** Create columns for categories like To Do, In Progress, and Done.
*   **Simple Interface:** Drag and drop tasks between columns.
*   **Team Sync:** Updates show up for every user in real-time.
*   **Search:** Find tasks with the search bar.
*   **Due Dates:** Set dates for task deadlines.
*   **Color Codes:** Assign colors to help you sort tasks by priority.

## 🚀 Getting Started

When you launch the program for the first time, you reach the main screen. The center space acts as your workspace.

### Create a Board
Select the New Board button at the top of the interface. Give your board a name, such as "Weekly Sprint" or "Client Projects."

### Add Tasks
Click the plus sign beneath a column to add a task. Type the name of the task and press Enter. You can click on the task later to add details, due dates, or descriptions.

### Move Tasks
Click and hold a task card. Drag the card to a new column to update its status. The software saves this move immediately for all team members.

## 💡 Usage Tips

*   **Use Descriptive Names:** Name tasks clearly so team members identify the goal at a glance.
*   **Prioritize Often:** Move urgent tasks to the top of your list so they stay visible.
*   **Check Due Dates:** Look for the date indicators on task cards to ensure you plan your day well.
*   **Refresh:** If you suspect you missed an update, press the Refresh button in the top menu.

## 🛡 Security and Privacy

Your data stays secure. The application uses encryption to send task information between your computer and the server. No outside party views your task lists. We store your account settings locally on your machine. You control who sees your boards through the settings menu.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Does the software work offline?**
Yes, you can add tasks while offline. The software syncs your changes once you regain an internet connection.

**Can I invite teammates?**
Yes. Open the settings menu, select Team, and type the email address of your teammate. They receive an invite to join your board.

**Does it require a paid subscription?**
This software is free to use for individual and team projects.

**How do I update the software?**
When a new version exists, the software displays a notification on the home screen. Click the notification, and the software downloads the update for you.

## 🛠 Support

If you encounter issues, look through the settings menu for a Help section. It contains links to common troubleshooting steps. If the issue remains, check your network connection first. Most errors occur because the computer fails to reach the server. Ensure your antivirus software allows the application to access the internet. You can uninstall and reinstall the software if errors persist.
